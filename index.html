About Chef Christian & Ballymagarvey Village

Exquisite cuisine for an unforgettable wedding day

Our philosophy

At Ballymagarvey Village, I believe that exceptional food lies at the heart of every celebration. Together with my talented culinary team, I’m dedicated to creating unforgettable dining experiences that blend creativity, passion, and the finest locally sourced ingredients.

The wedding feast

From delicate canapés to your elegant five-course wedding banquet, every dish is crafted and presented with care, showcasing both flavor and artistry.

The setting

Set within the timeless charm of Ballymagarvey’s dining spaces, your guests will savor a memorable feast — elevated by attentive service, refined presentation, and the warmth that defines this special place.
